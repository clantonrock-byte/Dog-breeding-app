What is our goal today?

One focus at a time keeps everything else from competing.
Records remain available for review or export. Voice may be used at any time.
Sections are skimmable by default. Deeper detail appears only when requested.

Dogs & Pups

A central record for each dog or pup.

Care Timeline

A time-stamped record of actions taken and observations noted.
This records what occurred. Information remains available for review or export.

Feeding

Records feeding-related information and notes.

Inventory

Inventory records what is on hand and how it changes.
Inventory adjusts only when a real event is recorded: added, used, discarded, retired, or transferred.
Any item with a barcode or QR code may be tracked. Thresholds are optional. Low items display in yellow.

Helpers

A shared view for coordination and support.

Records & Export

Information recorded across the app, available for review or export.
